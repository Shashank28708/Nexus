Overview

Frontend Learning Overview

Learning frontend development typically takes 2 to 3 months of consistent practice if you dedicate around 1–2 hours daily.

This roadmap is designed for 5 days of kickstart learning — giving you a quick start to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After this, continue building projects, explore frameworks (like React), and keep practicing.

Remember: real learning comes from building!
